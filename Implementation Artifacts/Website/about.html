About

Feedbook supports any RSS and Atom news feeds, It can also download podcasts saliently and play it within Feedbook.
It integrates with Twitter and Google Buzz very well, you can send tweets/post easily and can also subscribe to twitter timelines/buzz feeds. Characters like # & @ also recognize as special keywords, so that you can subscribe to feeds of any on going topic on twitter like #download, #movie or #cricket.
